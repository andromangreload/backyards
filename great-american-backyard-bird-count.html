Great American Backyard Bird Count. The great backyard bird count gbbc is a free fun and easy event that engages bird watchers of all ages in counting birds to create a real time snapshot of bird populations. Participants are asked to count birds for as little as 15 minutes or as long as they wish on one or more days of the four day event and report their sightings online.

The event is co sponsored by the cornell lab of ornithology and the national audubon society. The gbbc was one of the first demonstrations that the internet could be used to collect bird checklists and was instrumental in the creation of ebird. It s a citizen science endeavor organized by the national audubon society cornell laboratory of ornithology and birds canada.
